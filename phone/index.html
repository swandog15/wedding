Once upon a time...

 

...there was a girl who always wanted to work in the movie business. After graduating college, she got a job at a 3D film production company. One day, she overheard a boy passionately discussing a film project he was working on and instantly became infatuated. Over the next few months, she dropped hints that they should hang out until he eventually invited her out to Monday night bowling. The two became friends and traveled often, working on film shoots and attending 3D film festivals. They had many amazing adventures, and after a year of friendship he finally kissed her at a Halloween party!

 

A little over a year later, Ariela and Andrew moved to Los Angeles to pursue their careers in film. They continued their travels, trekking around Europe, the Caribbean and all over the US. They joined a bowling league, explored what LA had to offer, and staked out all the pizza and sushi restaurants in Southern California. One sunny day, they went to the Getty Museum and Andrew convinced Ariela to go to the roof of one of the buildings to take pictures. His plan was to propose there, but he got so nervous he proposed in the stairway instead! She of course said yes, and the wedding plans began...

 

We feel very lucky to have such wonderful friends and family, and we are so happy to share our story with you all. We look forward to celebrating with you and toasting to the next chapter of our journey together!
